About Me

Hi, I’m Tanmay, a passionate and dedicated final-year BCA student with a strong interest in web development and software engineering. I am on a journey to become a full-stack developer, specializing in creating dynamic and efficient web applications. Over the past few years, I have worked on various projects that allowed me to develop skills in both front-end and back-end technologies. My expertise lies in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript for building responsive and interactive user interfaces. Additionally, I have experience with frameworks like React.js and Angular, which enable me to create modern, single-page applications. On the back end, I am proficient in Node.js, Express, and working with databases such as MySQL and MongoDB, allowing me to build robust server-side applications. I am constantly learning and improving my skills, and I take pride in writing clean, maintainable, and efficient code. My interest in problem-solving extends beyond coding – I enjoy optimizing workflows and delivering user-friendly solutions. When I’m not coding, you’ll likely find me exploring new technologies, learning about the latest industry trends, or reading tech blogs to stay updated.
